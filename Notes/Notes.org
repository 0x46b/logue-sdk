* General Notes
** Formulas
- sin(x) => full oscillation every 2\pi steps
- 1Hz Sinewave => sin(2\pi*x)
  - e.g.: 440 Hz (A4) => sin(2\pi*x*440)
  - that means:
    - <Note>*\sqrt[12]{2} => One octave up
    - <Note>/\sqrt[12]{2} => One octave down
